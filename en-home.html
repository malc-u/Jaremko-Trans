About

Marek Jaremko, experienced HGV driver, founded Jaremko-Transport in 2016.

From the beginning he decided to offer professional transport services that will make him unique on the market. This is - the bespoke approach to each individual client. Company is focused to offer services that are best suited to the customer needs. Once order is placed the company makes sure that customer is informed about who oversees their business, what is the best form of communication and the cost of the service provided.

During the order process each client can contact the company and be assured that they will be informed which stage their order has progressed to.
